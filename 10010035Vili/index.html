About us

Do you ever find yourself working and have no time to go to the tech store after work? Have you ever noticed that there is a lack of tech shops open at night time? Night time is usually the main time that gamers have fun and fool around with their friends and this usually end up going through to early hours of the morning. Well that’s where AMTech come into play and offer night time services and will be available to the customers that work late and don’t have the luxury to go during the day time when the other tech companies are open. We will be located in Brookfield which is central in Tauranga and not to far to travel from the city centre and you will be able to come in from 12am – 5am and will be available online 24/7 as well.

Background

We have noticed a lack of technology stores open after hours, so we came up with idea to open a store that is open when the majority of the heavy gamers or even just the casual gamer getting of work and the local tech places are just closing. Now you’re not able to get the assistance you need. That’s where AMTech comes in.

Our Mission

To be available when all the other tech stores are closed. Give assistance to people that aren’t that tech savvy. Offer something different that most tech stores don,t offer. Sell mainly tech equipment like pc components, computers, Monitors, connectors, cables etc to those who don’t have the time during daytime.
